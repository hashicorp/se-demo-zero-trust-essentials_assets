Dynamic Secrets

Vault dynamic secrets makes it incredibly difficult for threat actors to escalate their privileges. Vault has the unique ability to create, rotate, and revoke secrets dynamically. These dynamic secrets can be generated on-demand, providing just-in-time credentials that are short-lived, along with being able to revoke access, and continuously rotate secrets.
