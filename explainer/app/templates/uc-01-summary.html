Summary of benefits

Approach Benefits
Establish standardization: Use standardized machine images to resolve the practices disparities. Teams are neither ahead nor behind in technology choices. And, an intentional synchronization saves time with testing cycles and minimizes edge cases that may undermine the overall health of the final deliverable.
Enforce security: Security teams can enforce audit and security protocols before the machine images are released for consumption. Infosec can automate testing on the machine image definitions to confirm adherence to protocols required by the organization.
Optimize efficiency: Teams do not have to make *every* technology decision for their images, only the ones that matter to them. The organization can onboard employees faster and delegate duties more efficiently for better business productivity.
